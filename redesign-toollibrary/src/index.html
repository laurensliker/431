About Us

SNTL is a nonprofit tool-lending library and community hub in the heart of Baltimore City. We offer over 3,000 tools, 30 classes, a public woodworking shop, a dedicated DIY workspace, and a home repair classroom.

Our space is a welcoming environment where people can be creative, foster a do-it-yourself attitude, and learn to work with their hands. We share what we know and learn from each other!
